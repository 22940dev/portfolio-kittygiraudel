About me

Hi! I'm Hugo, a French front-end developer at Edenspiekermann, in Berlin.

I have been writing for SitePoint, Tuts+, Web Design Weekly, The Sass Way, Codrops, CSS-tricks, Mixture and David Walsh' blog.

I am maintainer of SassDoc, writer of Sass Guidelines, curator of Sass-Compatibility, maker of Browserhacks and author of a lot of Sass stuff. Also, I wrote a book about CSS3 (in French).

You can catch me on Twitter, or have a look at my projects on GitHub.
